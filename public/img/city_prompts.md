# City Generation Prompts for "Nano Banana Pro"

Use these prompts to generate consistent, cute, 3D isometric city representations for your project.

**Global Style Settings:**
> **Style:** Cute, tiny, isometric 3D render. Claymorphism / High-end Toy aesthetic. Smooth textures, rounded edges, soft plastic look.
> **Composition:** Isolated on a **perfectly square** floating island base. The entire representation should form a **regular cube**. Clean white background.
> **Lighting:** Bright, soft studio lighting, global illumination, soft shadows, sunny and cheerful.
> **Camera:** Isometric view, high angle, macro photography feel (tilt-shift).
> **Quality:** 8k resolution, highly detailed, Octane Render.
